Things I believe

  • Now is the best time to be alive
  • Humans should build a civilisation on Mars
  • Humans are the only animal with a capacity for morality outside our own kin. We should cultivate this towards other people and animals.
  • The two top moral concerns: (1) the way we treat animals and (2) we have not globally eradicated preventable diseases
  • The world has gotten much better and continues to be so
  • Developing smaller, cheaper, simpler and lower-maintenance tunnel boring is highly underrated
  • The ultimate bottleneck for Europe's future is cultivating and attracting ultra-ambitious founders
  • Minimalism has peaked — beauty and tradition in architecture will make a comeback

How to improve conversations

  • Ask questions, be genuinely curious above all else
  • Tolerate silence, even if it's mildly uncomfortable
  • When you disagree, instead of stating your opinion ask: what's the best argument against this? Or: how would you summarise your opponent's position as charitably as possible?
  • Raise someone's ambitions. It's free, it's underrated and it works.

Startup insights

  • Founder obsession is the most important thing
  • Every startup should be like a cult with a secret at its core
  • Embrace pain, confusion and messiness
  • Iterate fast. Share the lessons.
  • Ego is the enemy. Make a point of writing down your mistakes to the group.
  • Present information starting with the conclusion
  • You can just do things. Ownership is how you rise.
  • Always question the requirements
  • Delete parts or processes — humans are hardwired to add, not remove
  • Celebrate wins — it signals what you value
  • Say the things no one else wants to say
  • Be nice

Principles for living
